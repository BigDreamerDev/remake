# Lighthouse Irregularity SW-MAR-4021

station: Harbour Lamp 0
keeper: Quill
review-cycle: night

## Beacon Behaviour

The beacon emitted 14 flashes during a period when power to the lamp was confirmed
disconnected. Observers at sea reported the flashes as visible only when looking away from
the tower. Inland observers reported the light reflecting from puddles inside locked rooms.
The keeper's log contained tomorrow's weather written in salt.
## Keeper Instructions

- Buckets must not be used for collection unless the bucket was dry before sunrise.
- Do not record your own name in a wet logbook.
- If a hallway smells of kelp, count the doors but do not open the third.
- Do not enter water that reflects a ceiling not present above you.
- If a window shows open sea where a wall is expected, draw the curtain and file Form MW-13.
