## Summary

At Grey Buoy Field, staff recorded a floor-level surf event lasting between 89 minutes and 7
hours. The occurrence was initially treated as a maintenance failure, then reclassified when

## Initial Report

The first report describes a clean smell of salt in a closed interior space. Personnel
present stated that the sound of distant surf became audible before any visible moisture
appeared. The nearest exterior waterline was logged as 494 metres away, though the incident
room later showed barnacle marks at ankle height.
One witness noted that the reflection in the water showed a wider room. No witness was able
to agree on whether the reflected space was frightening or familiar. This disagreement is
considered part of the event, not a failure of interviewing.
