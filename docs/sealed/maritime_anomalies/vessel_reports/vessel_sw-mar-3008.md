# Vessel Discrepancy Report SW-MAR-3008

vessel: M.V. Long Corridor
location: North Glass Shoal
status: name removed

## Log Abstract

The vessel was reported at North Glass Shoal after failing to match harbour departure
records. Its hull was wet above the painted waterline and dry below it. Crew lists provided
by harbour staff included 8 names, none of which were found in personnel databases, although
three appeared in old cafeteria seating plans.
A map recovered from the chart room showed corridors instead of sea lanes.
