# Vessel Discrepancy Report SW-MAR-3022

vessel: M.V. Hush Current
location: Crescent Breakwater
status: name removed

## Log Abstract

The vessel was reported at Crescent Breakwater after failing to match harbour departure
records. Its hull was wet above the painted waterline and dry below it. Crew lists provided
by harbour staff included 5 names, none of which were found in personnel databases, although
three appeared in old cafeteria seating plans.
The anchor chain was paid out into air and returned covered in black sand.

- All bunks were made, damp, and warm.
- The engine room smelled of rain on concrete.
- The cargo manifest listed chairs, salt, and one apologetic window.
- The galley clock displayed low tide instead of time.

## Disposition

redirection record and does not assign access privileges.
