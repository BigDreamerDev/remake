## Log Abstract

records. Its hull was wet above the painted waterline and dry below it. Crew lists provided
by harbour staff included 6 names, none of which were found in personnel databases, although
three appeared in old cafeteria seating plans.
The ship's wake travelled ahead of the bow and arrived at the quay before the vessel did.
