# Vessel Discrepancy Report SW-MAR-3006

vessel: M.V. Long Corridor
location: North Glass Shoal
status: returned unmanned

## Log Abstract

The vessel was reported at North Glass Shoal after failing to match harbour departure
records. Its hull was wet above the painted waterline and dry below it. Crew lists provided
by harbour staff included 4 names, none of which were found in personnel databases, although
three appeared in old cafeteria seating plans.
The ship's wake travelled ahead of the bow and arrived at the quay before the vessel did.

maritime redirection record and does not assign access privileges.
