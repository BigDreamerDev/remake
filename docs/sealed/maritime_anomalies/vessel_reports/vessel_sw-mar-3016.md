# Vessel Discrepancy Report SW-MAR-3016

vessel: Survey Boat Bellmouth
location: Ashen Ferry Terminal
status: seen twice

## Log Abstract

The vessel was reported at Ashen Ferry Terminal after failing to match harbour departure
records. Its hull was wet above the painted waterline and dry below it. Crew lists provided
by harbour staff included 5 names, none of which were found in personnel databases, although
three appeared in old cafeteria seating plans.
The anchor chain was paid out into air and returned covered in black sand.
## Boarding Notes

- All bunks were made, damp, and warm.
- Cabin doors opened onto the same narrow hallway regardless of deck level.
- The galley clock displayed low tide instead of time.
- All bunks were made, damp, and warm.
- The compass needle pointed toward the nearest closed door.
- No reflection was visible in the brass speaking tube.

## Disposition

Recommended action: send duplicate to the harbour that is not on any map. This report is a
maritime redirection record and does not assign access privileges.
