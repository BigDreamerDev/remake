# Vessel Discrepancy Report SW-MAR-3021

vessel: M.V. Long Corridor
location: Grey Buoy Field
status: seen twice

## Log Abstract

The vessel was reported at Grey Buoy Field after failing to match harbour departure records.
Its hull was wet above the painted waterline and dry below it. Crew lists provided by
harbour staff included 3 names, none of which were found in personnel databases, although
three appeared in old cafeteria seating plans.
The anchor chain was paid out into air and returned covered in black sand.
