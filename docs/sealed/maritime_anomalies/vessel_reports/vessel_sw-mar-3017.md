# Vessel Discrepancy Report SW-MAR-3017

vessel: The Dry Wake
location: Ashen Ferry Terminal
status: never departed

## Log Abstract

The vessel was reported at Ashen Ferry Terminal after failing to match harbour departure
records. Its hull was wet above the painted waterline and dry below it. Crew lists provided
by harbour staff included 6 names, none of which were found in personnel databases, although
three appeared in old cafeteria seating plans.
The ship's wake travelled ahead of the bow and arrived at the quay before the vessel did.

- The engine room smelled of rain on concrete.

## Disposition

Recommended action: file under 'ordinary condensation' until it spells a name. This report
is a maritime redirection record and does not assign access privileges.
