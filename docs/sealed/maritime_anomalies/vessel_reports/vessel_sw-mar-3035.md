# Vessel Discrepancy Report SW-MAR-3035

vessel: Survey Boat Bellmouth
location: Grey Buoy Field
status: kept offshore

## Log Abstract

The vessel was reported at Grey Buoy Field after failing to match harbour departure records.
Its hull was wet above the painted waterline and dry below it. Crew lists provided by
harbour staff included 6 names, none of which were found in personnel databases, although
three appeared in old cafeteria seating plans.
A radio message repeated the phrase 'the tide has already signed in' for eleven minutes.
## Boarding Notes

- All bunks were made, damp, and warm.
- The engine room smelled of rain on concrete.
- The galley clock displayed low tide instead of time.
- The compass needle pointed toward the nearest closed door.
- The cargo manifest listed chairs, salt, and one apologetic window.

## Disposition

Recommended action: seal the intake key in wax. This report is a maritime redirection record
and does not assign access privileges.
