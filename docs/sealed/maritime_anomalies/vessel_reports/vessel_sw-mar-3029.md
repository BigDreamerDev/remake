# Vessel Discrepancy Report SW-MAR-3029

vessel: M.V. Long Corridor
location: open water not on chart
status: returned unmanned

## Log Abstract

The vessel was reported at open water not on chart after failing to match harbour departure
records. Its hull was wet above the painted waterline and dry below it. Crew lists provided
by harbour staff included 9 names, none of which were found in personnel databases, although
three appeared in old cafeteria seating plans.
A map recovered from the chart room showed corridors instead of sea lanes.
## Boarding Notes

- Cabin doors opened onto the same narrow hallway regardless of deck level.
- The galley clock displayed low tide instead of time.
- The compass needle pointed toward the nearest closed door.
- All bunks were made, damp, and warm.
- No reflection was visible in the brass speaking tube.

## Disposition

Recommended action: seal the intake key in wax. This report is a maritime redirection record
and does not assign access privileges.
