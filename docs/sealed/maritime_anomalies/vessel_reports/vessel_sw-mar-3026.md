# Vessel Discrepancy Report SW-MAR-3026

vessel: Survey Boat Bellmouth
location: Crescent Breakwater
status: moored indoors

## Log Abstract

The vessel was reported at Crescent Breakwater after failing to match harbour departure
records. Its hull was wet above the painted waterline and dry below it. Crew lists provided
by harbour staff included 8 names, none of which were found in personnel databases, although
three appeared in old cafeteria seating plans.
The deck bell rang once every time an observer tried to count the lifeboats.

- No reflection was visible in the brass speaking tube.
- The galley clock displayed low tide instead of time.

## Disposition

Recommended action: compare against the cafeteria tide board. This report is a maritime
redirection record and does not assign access privileges.
