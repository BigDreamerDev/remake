# Vessel Discrepancy Report SW-MAR-3033

vessel: The Dry Wake
location: North Glass Shoal
status: never departed

## Log Abstract

The vessel was reported at North Glass Shoal after failing to match harbour departure
records. Its hull was wet above the painted waterline and dry below it. Crew lists provided
by harbour staff included 2 names, none of which were found in personnel databases, although
three appeared in old cafeteria seating plans.
A map recovered from the chart room showed corridors instead of sea lanes.
## Boarding Notes

- The compass needle pointed toward the nearest closed door.
- Cabin doors opened onto the same narrow hallway regardless of deck level.
- The engine room smelled of rain on concrete.
- The cargo manifest listed chairs, salt, and one apologetic window.
- The galley clock displayed low tide instead of time.

## Disposition

Recommended action: do not escalate without two dry witnesses. This report is a maritime
redirection record and does not assign access privileges.
