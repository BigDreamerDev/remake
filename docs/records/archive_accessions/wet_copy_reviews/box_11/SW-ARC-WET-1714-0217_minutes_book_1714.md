## Catalogue Note

The item was received as a minutes book and is treated as a historical document for the purposes of Codicology review. The recorded date is 1714, though the document has been known to prefer adjacent years when stored in damp shelving.

Condition on intake: waterline visible at lower margin.

The document should not be cleaned, flattened, warmed, sung near, or compared directly against any recollection supplied by the donor.

## Historical Summary

The lower margin contains a waterline that does not correspond to any known flood level.

The catalogue number was assigned before the accession arrived, according to the receipt book.

A historical seal appears in the correct position, but the impression is reversed, as if pressed from beneath the page.

The document references Locked Reading Room as if it were a civic location rather than an internal Foundation designation.
