## Catalogue Note

The item was received as a canal permit and is treated as a historical document for the purposes of Codicology review. The recorded date is 1488, though the document has been known to prefer adjacent years when stored in damp shelving.

The document should not be cleaned, flattened, warmed, sung near, or compared directly against any recollection supplied by the donor.

## Historical Summary

A witness named in the margin appears in two separate centuries of the archive without change of address.

The lower margin contains a waterline that does not correspond to any known flood level.

The document references East Wing as if it were a civic location rather than an internal Foundation designation.

A historical seal appears in the correct position, but the impression is reversed, as if pressed from beneath the page.
