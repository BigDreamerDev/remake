## Catalogue Note

The item was received as a estate ledger and is treated as a historical document for the purposes of Codicology review. The recorded date is 1666, though the document has been known to prefer adjacent years when stored in damp shelving.

Condition on intake: signature duplicated after drying.

The document should not be cleaned, flattened, warmed, sung near, or compared directly against any recollection supplied by the donor.

## Historical Summary

The lower margin contains a waterline that does not correspond to any known flood level.

Two folios use the same page number. Both have been treated as the original for different audits.

The document references Saltmarsh as if it were a civic location rather than an internal Foundation designation.

The item has been requested by a reading room that is not currently open to staff.
