## Catalogue Note

The item was received as a school attendance ledger and is treated as a historical document for the purposes of Codicology review. The recorded date is 1907, though the document has been known to prefer adjacent years when stored in damp shelving.

Condition on intake: ink blooms inward from gutter.

The document should not be cleaned, flattened, warmed, sung near, or compared directly against any recollection supplied by the donor.

## Historical Summary

The document references Black Shelf as if it were a civic location rather than an internal Foundation designation.

A historical seal appears in the correct position, but the impression is reversed, as if pressed from beneath the page.

The lower margin contains a waterline that does not correspond to any known flood level.

The item has been requested by a reading room that is not currently open to staff.
