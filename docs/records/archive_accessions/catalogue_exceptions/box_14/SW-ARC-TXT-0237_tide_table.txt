## Catalogue Note

The item was received as a tide table and is treated as a historical document for the purposes of Codicology review. The recorded date is 1541, though the document has been known to prefer adjacent years when stored in damp shelving.

Condition on intake: pagination continues beyond final folio.

The document should not be cleaned, flattened, warmed, sung near, or compared directly against any recollection supplied by the donor.

## Historical Summary

The catalogue number was assigned before the accession arrived, according to the receipt book.

A historical seal appears in the correct position, but the impression is reversed, as if pressed from beneath the page.

The document references Sublevel 5 as if it were a civic location rather than an internal Foundation designation.

The lower margin contains a waterline that does not correspond to any known flood level.

## Handling
