## Catalogue Note

The item was received as a school attendance ledger and is treated as a historical document for the purposes of Codicology review. The recorded date is 1666, though the document has been known to prefer adjacent years when stored in damp shelving.

Condition on intake: marginalia appears under oblique light.

The document should not be cleaned, flattened, warmed, sung near, or compared directly against any recollection supplied by the donor.

## Historical Summary

A witness named in the margin appears in two separate centuries of the archive without change of address.

The catalogue number was assigned before the accession arrived, according to the receipt book.

The item has been requested by a reading room that is not currently open to staff.

The document references Cresswell Quay as if it were a civic location rather than an internal Foundation designation.
