## Catalogue Note

The item was received as a sanatorium visitor book and is treated as a historical document for the purposes of Codicology review. The recorded date is 1666, though the document has been known to prefer adjacent years when stored in damp shelving.

Condition on intake: witness marks do not agree with index.

The document should not be cleaned, flattened, warmed, sung near, or compared directly against any recollection supplied by the donor.

## Historical Summary

The catalogue number was assigned before the accession arrived, according to the receipt book.

A docket note refers to the blank visitor entry. This may be administrative noise.

A historical seal appears in the correct position, but the impression is reversed, as if pressed from beneath the page.

The document references Lower Switchboard as if it were a civic location rather than an internal Foundation designation.

## Handling

- If the record becomes heavier after transcription, stop weighing it.
