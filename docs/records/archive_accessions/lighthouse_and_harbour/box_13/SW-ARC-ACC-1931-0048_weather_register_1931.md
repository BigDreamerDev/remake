## Catalogue Note

The item was received as a weather register and is treated as a historical document for the purposes of Codicology review. The recorded date is 1931, though the document has been known to prefer adjacent years when stored in damp shelving.

Condition on intake: witness marks do not agree with index.

The document should not be cleaned, flattened, warmed, sung near, or compared directly against any recollection supplied by the donor.

## Historical Summary

The document references Black Shelf as if it were a civic location rather than an internal Foundation designation.

The lower margin contains a waterline that does not correspond to any known flood level.

The catalogue number was assigned before the accession arrived, according to the receipt book.

A witness named in the margin appears in two separate centuries of the archive without change of address.
