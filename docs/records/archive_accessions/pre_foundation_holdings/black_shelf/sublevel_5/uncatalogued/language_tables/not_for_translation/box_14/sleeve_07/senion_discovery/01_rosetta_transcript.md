## Your working slip — Form SW-VOC-01

A reminder of the format the working group expects on returned slips:

```
SENION TOKEN     |  YOUR PROPOSED GLOSS     |  COLUMN(S) IT WAS ALIGNED FROM
-----------------+---------------------------+--------------------------------
```

A token that is bound to the noun (suffix) is recorded with a leading hyphen, as `-eth`. A token that stands alone is recorded without. The working group reads bare hyphens as bound morphemes; do not omit them.
