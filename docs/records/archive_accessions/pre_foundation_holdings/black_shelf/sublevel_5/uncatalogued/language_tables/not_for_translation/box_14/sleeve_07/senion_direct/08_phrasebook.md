# 08 — Phrasebook

Worked translations of common Foundation sentence types. Use as templates.

## Existence and presence

| English | Senion |
|---|---|
| The door exists. (I see it.) | **Kor han-nai.** |
| The door exists. (The record says so.) | **Kor han-seln.** |
| The door does not exist. (I infer it.) | **Kor unhan-thur.** |
| The witness is in the archive. (I see them.) | **Osher arvel-eth han-nai.** |
| Silence exists in the archive room. (Per the record.) | **Sel arvelul-eth han-seln.** |

## Movement

| English | Senion |
|---|---|
| The signal comes from below. (I hear it.) | **Sūn nēr-aru nadi-or-nal.** |
| The witness will go to the door. (I infer.) | **Osher kor-toh par-thel-thur.** |
| Three witnesses enter the room. (I see them.) | **Osher thren-mor mal-toh pareth-or-nai.** |
| The archivist comes from the archive. (Per the record.) | **Arveler arvel-aru nadi-or-seln.** |

## Past actions

| English | Senion |
|---|---|
| The door opened. (I saw it.) | **Kor morh-us-ren-nai.** |
| The witness read the record. (I saw.) | **Osher kelhom thuri-us-ren-nai.** |
| The signal arrived. (I'm told.) | **Sūn nadi-us-ren-kel.** |
| The name was at the threshold. (Per the record.) | **Meren kor-eth han-ren-seln.** |

## Future and routine

| English | Senion |
|---|---|
| The office will open. (As usual.) | **Torhul morh-thel-mar.** |
| The witness will read the record. (Per the record.) | **Osher kelhom thuri-thel-seln.** |
| The archive will not open tomorrow. (I infer.) | **Arvel unmorh-thel-thur.** |

## Sealed claims

| English | Senion |
|---|---|
| The door is open. (Sealed; per the record.) | **Kor han-vor-seln.** |
| The signal arrived. (Sealed; I heard it.) | **Sūn nadi-us-ren-vor-nal.** |
| The witness's name is in the archive. (Sealed; per the record.) | **Osher-en meren arvel-eth han-vor-seln.** |
| The archive opened. (Sealed; I'm told.) | **Arvel morh-us-ren-vor-kel.** |

## Topic-marked statements

| English | Senion |
|---|---|
| As for the door, it is in the archive. (I see.) | **Kor-ha, arvel-eth han-nai.** |
| As for the witness, they read the record. (Per the record.) | **Osher-ha, kelhom thuri-or-seln.** |
| As for the archive, silence exists there. (Per the record.) | **Arvel-ha, sel han-seln.** |
| As for the name, it is at the threshold. (Sealed; per the record.) | **Meren-ha, kor-eth han-vor-seln.** |

## Questions

| English | Senion |
|---|---|
| Does the door exist? (Per the record?) | **Kor han-seln ma?** |
| Did the witness read the record? (You saw?) | **Osher kelhom thuri-us-ren-nai ma?** |
| Is there a sound? (Did you hear?) | **Sūn han-nal ma?** |
| Will the office open? (As usual?) | **Torhul morh-thel-mar ma?** |

## Quoted speech

| English | Senion |
|---|---|
| "The door opened," they said. | **«Kor morh-us-ren», kelh-us-ren-sai.** |
| "Do not translate the silence," the record says. | **«Sel unglosi-vor», kelh-us-ren-seln.** |

## Negation patterns

| English | Senion |
|---|---|
| The witness does not exist. (I infer.) | **Osher unhan-thur.** |
| The record says the door is not open. | **Kor unmorh-or-seln.** |
| A not-room exists in the archive. (Per the record.) | **Unmal arvel-eth han-seln.** |
| The mirror does not show the witness. (I see.) | **Mir osher unosh-or-nai.** |

## Longer sample

A near-handbook passage:

> **Arvelul-ha, sel han-seln.**
> **Ya kelhom arvel-eth thuri-us-ren-seln.**
> **Korven mal-eth han-thur.**
> **Na kor-toh pareth-thel-thur.**

> As for the archive room, silence exists. (Per the record.)
> I read the record in the archive. (Per the record.)
> A false door exists in the room. (I infer.)
> You will enter toward the door. (I infer.)
