## Catalogue Note

The item was received as a burial index and is treated as a historical document for the purposes of Codicology review. The recorded date is 1907, though the document has been known to prefer adjacent years when stored in damp shelving.

Condition on intake: corners remember being folded.

The document should not be cleaned, flattened, warmed, sung near, or compared directly against any recollection supplied by the donor.

## Historical Summary

The lower margin contains a waterline that does not correspond to any known flood level.

A docket note refers to the lower switchboard. This may be administrative noise.

The document references Sublevel 5 as if it were a civic location rather than an internal Foundation designation.

Two folios use the same page number. Both have been treated as the original for different audits.
