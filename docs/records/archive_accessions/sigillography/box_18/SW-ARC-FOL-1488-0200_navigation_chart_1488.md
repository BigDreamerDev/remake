**Accession:** SW-ARC-FOL-1488-0200
**Custody:** Lower Reading Room
**Current Location:** Mossgate
**Material:** rag paper
**Hand:** roundhand
**Status:** open

## Catalogue Note

The item was received as a navigation chart and is treated as a historical document for the purposes of Codicology review. The recorded date is 1488, though the document has been known to prefer adjacent years when stored in damp shelving.

Condition on intake: waterline visible at lower margin.

The document should not be cleaned, flattened, warmed, sung near, or compared directly against any recollection supplied by the donor.

## Historical Summary

The item has been requested by a reading room that is not currently open to staff.

The document references Pondroom as if it were a civic location rather than an internal Foundation designation.

Two folios use the same page number. Both have been treated as the original for different audits.

A historical seal appears in the correct position, but the impression is reversed, as if pressed from beneath the page.

## Handling

- The original is not to be requested unless the copy asks first.
- File copies separately from originals even when the original insists there is no copy.
- If the record becomes heavier after transcription, stop weighing it.
- Do not move this item to a brighter shelf to prove that the ink is ordinary.

## Extract

Transfer a copy to Wet Copy Intake and leave the original where it believes it is.
