## Catalogue Note

The item was received as a ward admission slip and is treated as a historical document for the purposes of Codicology review. The recorded date is 1541, though the document has been known to prefer adjacent years when stored in damp shelving.

Condition on intake: signature duplicated after drying.

The document should not be cleaned, flattened, warmed, sung near, or compared directly against any recollection supplied by the donor.

## Historical Summary

The document references Black Shelf as if it were a civic location rather than an internal Foundation designation.

The catalogue number was assigned before the accession arrived, according to the receipt book.

The item has been requested by a reading room that is not currently open to staff.

A docket note refers to the seventh shelf. This may be administrative noise.
