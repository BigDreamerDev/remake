## Catalogue Note

The item was received as a dock manifest and is treated as a historical document for the purposes of Codicology review. The recorded date is 1931, though the document has been known to prefer adjacent years when stored in damp shelving.

Condition on intake: ink blooms inward from gutter.

The document should not be cleaned, flattened, warmed, sung near, or compared directly against any recollection supplied by the donor.

## Historical Summary

The item has been requested by a reading room that is not currently open to staff.

The lower margin contains a waterline that does not correspond to any known flood level.

The catalogue number was assigned before the accession arrived, according to the receipt book.

The document references Building Δ as if it were a civic location rather than an internal Foundation designation.
