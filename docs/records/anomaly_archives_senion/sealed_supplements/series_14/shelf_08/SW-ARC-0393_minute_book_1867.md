# SW-ARC-0393 — selh-bōk (1867)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0393
**Ravel-turen:** yath-kelhom
**Tos-tieren:** arvelil
**Korsh-ring:** selveth
**Domul:** Arvelhand velth
**Ren-dā:** 1867
**Keth:** oshom-paper
**Pareth:** ringil nāsh

## Domi-vor

- Mornith sul-or-eth tos unoshom-seln.
- Pareth dū-mor karer en-tos shavven-dor han-seln.
- Dūkelh paper nāsh-dor kelhi-seln. Dūkelh unmorh-vor-seln.

## Savar

selh-bōk-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
selh-savar ren-toh par-us-seln.
Ren-dā 1867 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Todom ven han-ren-seln; tos todom-eth han-us-ren-nai.

## Incidom

Dūkelh keshul sēlil-eth han-us-ren-nai. Incidom dom-seln.

## Yath-kelhom
