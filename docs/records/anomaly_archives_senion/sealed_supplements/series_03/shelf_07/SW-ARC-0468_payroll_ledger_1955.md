# SW-ARC-0468 — tier-dakelom (1955)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0468
**Ravel-turen:** dakel dovelom
**Tos-tieren:** formil
**Korsh-ring:** brakil
**Domul:** Arvel-sēl torh
**Ren-dā:** 1955
**Keth:** dakel-paper
**Pareth:** rūm-dor vornil

## Domi-vor

- Karer-pararu-vek-eth tos morh-or unhan-seln.
- Tos thurer mereni-kel; osh-domul-toh pararu-seln; kelh-vesh.
- Fol sen-eth yath-dor unthuri-seln.
- Tos folnūm doveli-ek-kel; ravel sēli-seln; marn-aru pareth-ek-seln.
- Tos besh-an dom-us-seln. Tos ravel-keth thuri unkelh-seln.

## Savar

tier-dakelom-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
tier ven-toh shelk-ek-seln.
Ren-dā 1955 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Ravel han-ren-oth ravel-eth han-us-ren-seln.

## Incidom

Thuriul mornith senpem-veth ven-us-ren-seln. Shelkom unmorh-ren-kel.

## Yath-kelhom

Mal nāsh shol-ren-nai. To dovel en-veth han-us-ren-sai.
