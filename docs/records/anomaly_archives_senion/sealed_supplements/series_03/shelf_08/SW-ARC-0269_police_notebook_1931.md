# SW-ARC-0269 — oshri-bōk (1931)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0269
**Ravel-turen:** mar-kelh thuriom
**Tos-tieren:** selil
**Korsh-ring:** brakil
**Domul:** formil dovel torh
**Ren-dā:** 1931
**Keth:** paper
**Pareth:** incidom-selh-toh sēlil

## Domi-vor

- Pareth dū-mor karer en-tos shavven-dor han-seln.
- Tos shar-lan kelh-kel; dakelni unkelh-seln.
- Tos folnūm doveli-ek-kel; ravel sēli-seln; marn-aru pareth-ek-seln.
- Karer-pararu-vek-eth tos morh-or unhan-seln.
- Tos thurer mereni-kel; osh-domul-toh pararu-seln; kelh-vesh.

## Savar

oshri-bōk-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
torhul-lan sēl-thel-oth savarni-ren-seln.
Ren-dā 1931 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Nadar shelkom kelh-us-ren-kel; nadar unkelh-ren-kel.

## Incidom

Renh-veth ven-seln. Karnel dū-om tier-dor dūmi-us-ren-seln.

## Yath-kelhom

Dūkelh-lan sēl-ren-sai; kelh en-om unsēl-ren-sai.
