# SW-ARC-0151 — kar-dakelom (1921)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0151
**Ravel-turen:** dakel dovelom
**Tos-tieren:** formil
**Korsh-ring:** nāsh
**Domul:** Arvel-sēl torh
**Ren-dā:** 1921
**Keth:** paper
**Pareth:** ringil nāsh

## Domi-vor

- Tos besh-an dom-us-seln. Tos ravel-keth thuri unkelh-seln.
- Tos shar-lan kelh-kel; dakelni unkelh-seln.
- Tos thurer mereni-kel; osh-domul-toh pararu-seln; kelh-vesh.
- Ret-sol formom-tor undomi-seln.

## Savar

kar-dakelom-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
shai-lan morh-ren-oth han-seln.
Ren-dā 1921 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Selh-vek-eth tos pareth-us-ren-seln.

## Incidom

Dūkelh keshul sēlil-eth han-us-ren-nai. Incidom dom-seln.

## Yath-kelhom

Lūn-lan unbraki-ren-sai. Brak-lan braki-ren-sai.
