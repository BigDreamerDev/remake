# SW-ARC-0160 — oshri-bōk (1931)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0160
**Ravel-turen:** dovelil arvel kelhom
**Tos-tieren:** dovelveth
**Korsh-ring:** selveth
**Domul:** rūm mereni torh
**Ren-dā:** 1931
**Keth:** oshom-paper
**Pareth:** rūm-dor vornil

## Domi-vor

- Tos shar-lan kelh-kel; dakelni unkelh-seln.
- Fol sen-eth yath-dor unthuri-seln.
- Dūkelh paper nāsh-dor kelhi-seln. Dūkelh unmorh-vor-seln.
- Mornith sul-or-eth tos unoshom-seln.

## Savar

oshri-bōk-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
torhul-lan sēl-thel-oth savarni-ren-seln.
Ren-dā 1931 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Nadar shelkom kelh-us-ren-kel; nadar unkelh-ren-kel.

## Incidom

Dūkelh keshul sēlil-eth han-us-ren-nai. Incidom dom-seln.

## Yath-kelhom

Tos nāsh han-ren-sai; kar tos savaril dovi-ren-theloth korsh han-ren-sai.
