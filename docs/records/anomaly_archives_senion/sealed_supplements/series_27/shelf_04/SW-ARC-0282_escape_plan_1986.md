# SW-ARC-0282 — pararu-dakelom (1986)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0282
**Ravel-turen:** oshom ravelom
**Tos-tieren:** tarveth
**Korsh-ring:** selveth
**Domul:** formil dovel torh
**Ren-dā:** 1986
**Keth:** oshom-paper
**Pareth:** rūm-dor vornil

## Domi-vor

- Tos folnūm doveli-ek-kel; ravel sēli-seln; marn-aru pareth-ek-seln.
- Tos shar-lan kelh-kel; dakelni unkelh-seln.
- Pareth dū-mor karer en-tos shavven-dor han-seln.
- Dūkelh paper nāsh-dor kelhi-seln. Dūkelh unmorh-vor-seln.

## Savar

pararu-dakelom-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
nethil vethrul osh-er-athel han-nai.
Ren-dā 1986 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Selh-vek-eth tos pareth-us-ren-seln.

## Incidom

Tos ravel-eth ret-us-ren-nai. Ravel oshveth-toh par-us-ren-seln.

## Yath-kelhom

Ya ravel sēli marn-ren-sai. Ya vehel morhi unmarn-ren-sai.

- SW-OBJ-CHAIR-113 kelhom-dor thuri-seln.
