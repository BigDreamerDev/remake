# SW-ARC-0469 — glass-oshom (1892)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0469
**Ravel-turen:** incidom savarom
**Tos-tieren:** retom
**Korsh-ring:** brakveth
**Domul:** renkelh doveli torhul
**Ren-dā:** 1892
**Keth:** paper
**Pareth:** karer-selh

## Domi-vor

- Tos shar-lan kelh-kel; dakelni unkelh-seln.
- Tos folnūm doveli-ek-kel; ravel sēli-seln; marn-aru pareth-ek-seln.
- Mornith sul-or-eth tos unoshom-seln.
- Karer-pararu-vek-eth tos morh-or unhan-seln.
- Fol sen-eth yath-dor unthuri-seln.

## Savar

glass-oshom-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
kar-lan dov-toh shol han-nai; haren unshol-thur.
Ren-dā 1892 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Dakel dovel-eth tos pareth-us-ren-seln.

## Incidom

Renh-veth ven-seln. Karnel dū-om tier-dor dūmi-us-ren-seln.

## Yath-kelhom

Lūn-lan unbraki-ren-sai. Brak-lan braki-ren-sai.
