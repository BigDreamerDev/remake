# SW-ARC-0371 — vethr-dakelom (1910)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0371
**Ravel-turen:** tos domi savarom
**Tos-tieren:** sulveth
**Korsh-ring:** sēlil
**Domul:** thuriul nēr-en
**Ren-dā:** 1910
**Keth:** oshom-paper
**Pareth:** karer-selh

## Domi-vor

- Lūn nāsh-bel dom-or-seln. Brakdā-thel kelhom-lan-dor unthuri-seln.
- Fol sen-eth yath-dor unthuri-seln.
- Tos folnūm doveli-ek-kel; ravel sēli-seln; marn-aru pareth-ek-seln.
- Tos thurer mereni-kel; osh-domul-toh pararu-seln; kelh-vesh.
- Mornith sul-or-eth tos unoshom-seln.

## Savar

vethr-dakelom-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
ravelveth thel-eth han-ek-thur.
Ren-dā 1910 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Nadar shelkom kelh-us-ren-kel; nadar unkelh-ren-kel.

## Incidom

Dūkelh keshul sēlil-eth han-us-ren-nai. Incidom dom-seln.

## Yath-kelhom

Kelhom unpar-ren-sai. Kesh yen-tor par-ren-sai.
