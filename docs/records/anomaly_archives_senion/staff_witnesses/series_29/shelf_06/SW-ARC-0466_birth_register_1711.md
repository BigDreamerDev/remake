# SW-ARC-0466 — shai-kelh codarom (1711)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0466
**Ravel-turen:** domveth kelhom
**Tos-tieren:** selil
**Korsh-ring:** meren-vesh
**Domul:** karer tarveth
**Ren-dā:** 1711
**Keth:** oshom-paper
**Pareth:** rūm-dor vornil

## Domi-vor

- Dūkelh paper nāsh-dor kelhi-seln. Dūkelh unmorh-vor-seln.
- Tos thurer mereni-kel; osh-domul-toh pararu-seln; kelh-vesh.
- Pareth dū-mor karer en-tos shavven-dor han-seln.
- Tos shar-lan kelh-kel; dakelni unkelh-seln.

## Savar

shai-kelh codarom-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
shai-kelh en-om tar-ek-seln.
Ren-dā 1711 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Dakel dovel-eth tos pareth-us-ren-seln.

## Incidom

Dūkelh keshul sēlil-eth han-us-ren-nai. Incidom dom-seln.

## Yath-kelhom

Ya yath unnal-ren-sai. Ya dakelni kelh nal-ren-sai.
