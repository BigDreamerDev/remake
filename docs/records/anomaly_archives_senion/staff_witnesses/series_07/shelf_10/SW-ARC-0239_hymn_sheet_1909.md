# SW-ARC-0239 — sūn-folom (1909)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0239
**Ravel-turen:** dakel dovelom
**Tos-tieren:** tarveth
**Korsh-ring:** sēlil
**Domul:** Arvelhand velth
**Ren-dā:** 1909
**Keth:** glassil met-vesh
**Pareth:** rūm-dor vornil

## Domi-vor

- Tos shar-lan kelh-kel; dakelni unkelh-seln.
- Pareth dū-mor karer en-tos shavven-dor han-seln.
- Tos thurer mereni-kel; osh-domul-toh pararu-seln; kelh-vesh.
- Ret-sol formom-tor undomi-seln.
- Ravelom brakveth ravel-eth dom-us-seln. Glass-dor unsēli-seln.

## Savar

sūn-folom-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
sūn-fol en-om marn-vesh han-seln.
Ren-dā 1909 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Nadar shelkom kelh-us-ren-kel; nadar unkelh-ren-kel.

## Incidom

Thuriul mornith senpem-veth ven-us-ren-seln. Shelkom unmorh-ren-kel.

## Yath-kelhom

Shelkom han-ren-kel; ye ya-en ringnūm doveli-us-ren-sai.
