# SW-ARC-0172 — vehel formom (1966)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0172
**Ravel-turen:** yath-kelhom
**Tos-tieren:** ven
**Korsh-ring:** sēlil
**Domul:** osh-domul
**Ren-dā:** 1966
**Keth:** paper
**Pareth:** ringil nāsh

## Domi-vor

- Ret-sol formom-tor undomi-seln.
- Fol sen-eth yath-dor unthuri-seln.
- Lūn nāsh-bel dom-or-seln. Brakdā-thel kelhom-lan-dor unthuri-seln.
- Mornith sul-or-eth tos unoshom-seln.
- Tos shar-lan kelh-kel; dakelni unkelh-seln.

## Savar

vehel formom-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
beshbel en-ul thel-eth han-seln.
Ren-dā 1966 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

## Incidom

Dūkelh keshul sēlil-eth han-us-ren-nai. Incidom dom-seln.

## Yath-kelhom
