# SW-ARC-0173 — torhul merenom (2014)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0173
**Ravel-turen:** dakel dovelom
**Tos-tieren:** tarveth
**Korsh-ring:** brakil
**Domul:** tos-pareth torh
**Ren-dā:** 2014
**Keth:** oshom-paper
**Pareth:** incidom-selh-toh sēlil

## Domi-vor

- Pareth dū-mor karer en-tos shavven-dor han-seln.
- Mornith sul-or-eth tos unoshom-seln.
- Lūn nāsh-bel dom-or-seln. Brakdā-thel kelhom-lan-dor unthuri-seln.
- Ret-sol formom-tor undomi-seln.
- Tos shar-lan kelh-kel; dakelni unkelh-seln.

## Savar

torhul merenom-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
tier-lan oshri-vesh doveli-ek-thur.
Ren-dā 2014 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Todom ven han-ren-seln; tos todom-eth han-us-ren-nai.

## Incidom

Dūkelh keshul sēlil-eth han-us-ren-nai. Incidom dom-seln.

## Yath-kelhom

Ya yath unnal-ren-sai. Ya dakelni kelh nal-ren-sai.
