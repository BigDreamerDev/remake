# SW-ARC-0329 — form-bōk (1943)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0329
**Ravel-turen:** oshom ravelom
**Tos-tieren:** oshnai
**Korsh-ring:** sēlil
**Domul:** lampaith selh
**Ren-dā:** 1943
**Keth:** glassil met-vesh
**Pareth:** dū-ek unkelhi-seln

## Domi-vor

- Ravelom brakveth ravel-eth dom-us-seln. Glass-dor unsēli-seln.
- Tos thurer mereni-kel; osh-domul-toh pararu-seln; kelh-vesh.
- Mornith sul-or-eth tos unoshom-seln.
- Pareth dū-mor karer en-tos shavven-dor han-seln.
- Tos shar-lan kelh-kel; dakelni unkelh-seln.

## Savar

form-bōk-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
mal-lan-toh kavil han-seln.
Ren-dā 1943 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Todom ven han-ren-seln; tos todom-eth han-us-ren-nai.

## Incidom

Osh en-mor tos unmarn-ren-kel; renil cloth-en reth dom-or-ren-nai.

## Yath-kelhom

Shelkom han-ren-kel; ye ya-en ringnūm doveli-us-ren-sai.
