# SW-ARC-0142 — pararu-dakelom (1986)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0142
**Ravel-turen:** besh reportom
**Tos-tieren:** oshnai
**Korsh-ring:** selveth
**Domul:** thuriul nēr-en
**Ren-dā:** 1986
**Keth:** glassil met-vesh
**Pareth:** incidom-selh-toh sēlil

## Domi-vor

- Tos folnūm doveli-ek-kel; ravel sēli-seln; marn-aru pareth-ek-seln.
- Tos shar-lan kelh-kel; dakelni unkelh-seln.
- Fol sen-eth yath-dor unthuri-seln.

## Savar

pararu-dakelom-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
nethil vethrul osh-er-athel han-nai.
Ren-dā 1986 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Dakel dovel-eth tos pareth-us-ren-seln.

## Incidom

Lūn-lan unsūni-ren-eth incidom sēl-us-ren-seln. Mal kelh-us-ren-kel.

## Yath-kelhom

Tos nāsh han-ren-sai; kar tos savaril dovi-ren-theloth korsh han-ren-sai.

## Mar-kelh

- SW-ARCHIVE-VOID-77 kelhom-dor thuri-seln.
- SW-INC-STAIR-044 kelhom-dor thuri-seln.
