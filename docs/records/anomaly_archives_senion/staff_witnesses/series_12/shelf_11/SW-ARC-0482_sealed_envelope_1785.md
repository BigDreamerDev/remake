# SW-ARC-0482 — sēlil ravel (1785)

**Ravelom:** SW-ARC-0482
**Ravel-turen:** mar-kelh thuriom
**Tos-tieren:** oshnai
**Korsh-ring:** nāsh
**Domul:** thuriul nēr-en
**Ren-dā:** 1785
**Keth:** paper
**Pareth:** oshri-dor morh-seln

## Domi-vor

- Tos folnūm doveli-ek-kel; ravel sēli-seln; marn-aru pareth-ek-seln.
- Ret-sol formom-tor undomi-seln.
- Tos thurer mereni-kel; osh-domul-toh pararu-seln; kelh-vesh.
- Pareth dū-mor karer en-tos shavven-dor han-seln.
- Lūn nāsh-bel dom-or-seln. Brakdā-thel kelhom-lan-dor unthuri-seln.

## Savar

sēlil ravel-ha, dovelveth han-seln.
karer ven-en domel-markel han-seln.
Ren-dā 1785 han-seln. Dā-en savar tos-en dovelveth-aru ulth han-thur.
Tos unsēli-seln. Sēl-veth tos dūkelh-lan morh-ek-thur.

## Pareth-kelh

Nadar shelkom kelh-us-ren-kel; nadar unkelh-ren-kel.

## Incidom

Lūn-lan unsūni-ren-eth incidom sēl-us-ren-seln. Mal kelh-us-ren-kel.

## Yath-kelhom

Ya yath unnal-ren-sai. Ya dakelni kelh nal-ren-sai.
