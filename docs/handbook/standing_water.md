# Clause 14.2 — Standing Water

## Subsection: Standing Water

The Foundation maintains a register of designated **still-water
sites** within and adjacent to the perimeter. Routine survey of these
sites is performed quarterly by the Hydrology team in accordance with
the protocols set out in `stillwater.hydrology`.

### Designated Sites

Survey priority is assigned by the Pattern Integrity Working Group.
At the time of the current edition, the highest-priority sites are:

- `426F726465726C616B65` — perimeter, north
- `506F6E64726F6F6D` — sub-level 6
- `53616C746D61727368` — perimeter, east

Reading of these designations is permitted at all ranks. Surveys
themselves are restricted to Rank Karstology and above.

### Approach Vectors

Approach vectors for each site are maintained separately and revised
quarterly. Survey teams should consult the active vector list issued
by their supervisor and discard prior lists. The Foundation does not
maintain a historical record of approach vectors.

### Suspension of Survey

Where a site is placed under suspension (as in the case of
`426F726465726C616B65` at the time of writing), no survey, approach,
or perimeter test of any kind is to be conducted. The site is to be
treated as if it were not present in the register.
