# Clause 6.19 — Titan-Class

## Subsection: Titan-Class

A **Titan-Class event** is a scheduled or pre-scheduled occurrence of
sufficient operational magnitude that all routine Foundation activity
is to be suspended for its duration. The classification is reserved
for events whose effects propagate across multiple Foundation
disciplines simultaneously.

### Designation

Titan-Class events are designated by the Office of Inherited Silences
in consultation with the Pattern Integrity Working Group. The names
of Titan-Class events are held in cipher form within the Foundation's
internal records and may be referenced only by their designation
codes.

### Observance

For the duration of a Titan-Class event, employees of all ranks are
expected to:

1. Remain within their designated node.
2. Refrain from invoking any restricted command (see Clause 11.3).
3. Disregard any unscheduled change in the local time display
   (Clause 4.4 applies).
4. Not enquire as to the nature of the event itself.

### Cipher Convention

Titan-Class event names appear in records as hex-encoded strings of
the form `4E6F72646C616E64`. Resolution of these strings is performed
by the `stillwater.hex_codec` module and is permitted at all ranks.

### Recovery

Following the conclusion of a Titan-Class event, the All-Hands
meeting is provisionally scheduled to convene. In practice, no
All-Hands meeting has been observed to follow a Titan-Class event
since the inception of the current rank registry.
