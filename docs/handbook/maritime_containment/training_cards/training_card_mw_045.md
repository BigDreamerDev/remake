# Training Card: Sea Sounds Indoors

card-id: TC-MW-045
duration: 10 minutes

## Remember

- A dry room can be a wet room that has not admitted it yet.
- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.
- If the corridor sounds like surf, take the stairs only if the stairs are dry.
- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Ashen Ferry Terminal. A impossible depth occurs while the room is
occupied by 3 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave
without testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
