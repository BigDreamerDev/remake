# Training Card: Lighthouse Without Shore

card-id: TC-MW-025
duration: 7 minutes

## Remember

- The sea does not need permission to be symbolic.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.
- A dry room can be a wet room that has not admitted it yet.
- No bucket is neutral.
- The first witness is not always the first person present.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Old Bathing Pavilion. A mirror water occurs while the room is occupied
by 2 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without
testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
