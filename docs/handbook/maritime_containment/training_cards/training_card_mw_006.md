# Training Card: The Waterline Is Not A Border

card-id: TC-MW-006
duration: 13 minutes

## Remember

- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.
- If the corridor sounds like surf, take the stairs only if the stairs are dry.
- The sea does not need permission to be symbolic.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to The Third Jetty. A drowned corridor occurs while the room is occupied by
6 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without testing
whether the water is deep enough to matter.
