# Training Card: Lighthouse Without Shore

card-id: TC-MW-032
duration: 5 minutes

## Remember

- Not every tide moves horizontally.
- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.
- The sea does not need permission to be symbolic.
- No bucket is neutral.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Ward C Washroom. A eel-shaped shadow occurs while the room is occupied
by 8 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without
testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
