# Training Card: Mirrors Near Drains

card-id: TC-MW-021
duration: 10 minutes

## Remember

- If the corridor sounds like surf, take the stairs only if the stairs are dry.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.
- No bucket is neutral.
- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Room 0 Flood Annex. A salt apparition occurs while the room is occupied
by 2 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without
testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
