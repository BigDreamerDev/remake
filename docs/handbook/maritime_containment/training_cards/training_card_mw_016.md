# Training Card: Sea Sounds Indoors

card-id: TC-MW-016
duration: 3 minutes

## Remember

- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- No bucket is neutral.
- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.
- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to West Sluice Door. A indoor swell occurs while the room is occupied by 4
staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without testing
whether the water is deep enough to matter.
