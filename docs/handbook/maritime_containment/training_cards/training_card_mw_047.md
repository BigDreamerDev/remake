# Training Card: Sea Sounds Indoors

card-id: TC-MW-047
duration: 7 minutes

## Remember

- Not every tide moves horizontally.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.
- If the corridor sounds like surf, take the stairs only if the stairs are dry.
- The sea does not need permission to be symbolic.
- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Grey Buoy Field. A voice-carrying fog occurs while the room is occupied
by 6 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without
testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
