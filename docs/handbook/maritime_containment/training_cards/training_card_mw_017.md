# Training Card: Do Not Hum Back

card-id: TC-MW-017
duration: 16 minutes

## Remember

- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.
- A dry room can be a wet room that has not admitted it yet.
- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.
- The sea does not need permission to be symbolic.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Ward C Washroom. A submerged office occurs while the room is occupied by
7 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without testing
whether the water is deep enough to matter.
