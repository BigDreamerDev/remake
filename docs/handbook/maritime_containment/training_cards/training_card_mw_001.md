# Training Card: Do Not Hum Back

card-id: TC-MW-001
duration: 3 minutes

## Remember

- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.
- The sea does not need permission to be symbolic.
- No bucket is neutral.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to North Glass Shoal. A salt apparition occurs while the room is occupied
by 7 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without
testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
