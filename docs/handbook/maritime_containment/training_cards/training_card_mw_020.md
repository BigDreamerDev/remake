# Training Card: Do Not Hum Back

card-id: TC-MW-020
duration: 18 minutes

## Remember

- No bucket is neutral.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.
- A dry room can be a wet room that has not admitted it yet.
- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Undertow Corridor. A coastal hallway recursion occurs while the room is
occupied by 6 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave
without testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
