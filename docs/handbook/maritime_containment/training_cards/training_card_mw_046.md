# Training Card: Sea Sounds Indoors

card-id: TC-MW-046
duration: 14 minutes

## Remember

- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- If the corridor sounds like surf, take the stairs only if the stairs are dry.
- A dry room can be a wet room that has not admitted it yet.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.
- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Grey Buoy Field. A unreturning rain occurs while the room is occupied by
5 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without testing
whether the water is deep enough to matter.
