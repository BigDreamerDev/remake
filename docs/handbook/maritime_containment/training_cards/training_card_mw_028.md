# Training Card: Do Not Hum Back

card-id: TC-MW-028
duration: 9 minutes

## Remember

- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.
- If the corridor sounds like surf, take the stairs only if the stairs are dry.
- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.
- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- A dry room can be a wet room that has not admitted it yet.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to The Dry Dock With Water In It. A language in condensation occurs while
the room is occupied by 3 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and
leave without testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
