# Training Card: Lighthouse Without Shore

card-id: TC-MW-038
duration: 13 minutes

## Remember

- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.
- No bucket is neutral.
- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.
- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to The Pool That Was Not Built. A waterline displacement occurs while the
room is occupied by 5 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and
leave without testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
