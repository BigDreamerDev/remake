# Training Card: Mirrors Near Drains

card-id: TC-MW-019
duration: 17 minutes

## Remember

- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.
- A dry room can be a wet room that has not admitted it yet.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.
- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to North Glass Shoal. A eel-shaped shadow occurs while the room is occupied
by 3 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without
testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
