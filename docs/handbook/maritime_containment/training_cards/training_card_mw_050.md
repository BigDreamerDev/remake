# Training Card: Mirrors Near Drains

card-id: TC-MW-050
duration: 10 minutes

## Remember

- If the corridor sounds like surf, take the stairs only if the stairs are dry.
- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.
- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.
- No bucket is neutral.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Grey Buoy Field. A submerged office occurs while the room is occupied by
4 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without testing
whether the water is deep enough to matter.
