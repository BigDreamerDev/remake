# Training Card: Mirrors Near Drains

card-id: TC-MW-043
duration: 16 minutes

## Remember

- A dry room can be a wet room that has not admitted it yet.
- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.
- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.
- No bucket is neutral.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Lighthouse 7. A sleeping bell occurs while the room is occupied by 5
staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without testing
whether the water is deep enough to matter.
