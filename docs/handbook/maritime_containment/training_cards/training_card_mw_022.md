# Training Card: The Waterline Is Not A Border

card-id: TC-MW-022
duration: 14 minutes

## Remember

- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.
- If the corridor sounds like surf, take the stairs only if the stairs are dry.
- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- No bucket is neutral.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to North Glass Shoal. A tidal duplication occurs while the room is occupied
by 1 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without
testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
