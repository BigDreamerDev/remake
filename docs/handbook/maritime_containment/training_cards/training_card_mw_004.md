# Training Card: When The Room Has A Tide

card-id: TC-MW-004
duration: 5 minutes

## Remember

- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- A dry room can be a wet room that has not admitted it yet.
- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.
- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- If the corridor sounds like surf, take the stairs only if the stairs are dry.
- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Old Bathing Pavilion. A submerged office occurs while the room is
occupied by 8 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave
without testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
