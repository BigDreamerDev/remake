# Training Card: Sea Sounds Indoors

card-id: TC-MW-009
duration: 15 minutes

## Remember

- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.
- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- No bucket is neutral.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.
- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Crescent Breakwater. A salt apparition occurs while the room is occupied
by 1 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without
testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
