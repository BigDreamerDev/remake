# Training Card: Lighthouse Without Shore

card-id: TC-MW-048
duration: 5 minutes

## Remember

- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.
- The sea does not need permission to be symbolic.
- If the corridor sounds like surf, take the stairs only if the stairs are dry.
- The first witness is not always the first person present.
- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Oyster Stair. A language in condensation occurs while the room is
occupied by 5 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave
without testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
