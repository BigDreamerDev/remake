# Training Card: Lighthouse Without Shore

card-id: TC-MW-023
duration: 5 minutes

## Remember

- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.
- A dry room can be a wet room that has not admitted it yet.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.
- No bucket is neutral.
- If the corridor sounds like surf, take the stairs only if the stairs are dry.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to South Intake Tunnel. A floor-level surf occurs while the room is
occupied by 2 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave
without testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
