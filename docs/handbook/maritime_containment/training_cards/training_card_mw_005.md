# Training Card: When The Room Has A Tide

card-id: TC-MW-005
duration: 10 minutes

## Remember

- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- No bucket is neutral.
- A reflection may be an invitation. Decline quietly.
- Not every tide moves horizontally.
- Liminal water often behaves like a door with no handle.
- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to The Empty Aquarium. A stairwell flood without source occurs while the
room is occupied by 3 staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and
leave without testing whether the water is deep enough to matter.
