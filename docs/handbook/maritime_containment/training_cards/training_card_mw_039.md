# Training Card: The Waterline Is Not A Border

card-id: TC-MW-039
duration: 18 minutes

## Remember

- Wet paperwork should be photographed before it starts correcting itself.
- The sea does not need permission to be symbolic.
- If the corridor sounds like surf, take the stairs only if the stairs are dry.
- The safest answer to a bell is silence.
- A dry room can be a wet room that has not admitted it yet.
- No bucket is neutral.

## Scenario Drill

You are assigned to Ward C Washroom. A reverse tide occurs while the room is occupied by 7
staff. Write down the dry exit, cover any reflective surfaces, and leave without testing
whether the water is deep enough to matter.
